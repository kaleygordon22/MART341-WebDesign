Get To Know Me!

Welcome to Kaley's Coffee! My name is Kaley and this is my little blog showing all of my favorite coffees to make as well as some extra treats. I grew up in Napa, California, but my family moved us to Montana when I was around ten years old. I have wonderful friends, an incredible boyfriend, and a loving family that I cherish with my whole heart. I love painting, swimming, hiking, watching movies, baking, and I especially love coffee. I have this blog to share my work, while hoping I can inspire people to use these recipes and make delicious coffees for yourself!
