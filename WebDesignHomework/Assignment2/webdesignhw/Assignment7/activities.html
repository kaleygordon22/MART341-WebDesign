Homemade Pumpkin Spice Latte

Start your Autumn season off right with this delicious homemade pumpkin spice latte! Served hot or cold, it is a perfect treat to start your morning, a mid day snack, or even a late night treat. Customize with your own ingredients or follow this recipe exactly, and you are guaranteed a wonderful fall beverage

Ingredients:

  • Freshly Brewed Coffee: 1/2 cup of freshly brewed coffee, brew it however strong you like it
  • Milk: Heat up your choice of milk in a small pot until it is hot, but do not boil it
  • Pumpkin Puree: Take one can of pumpkin puree and use however much you would like. It is a personal preference on how pumpkin-flavored you would like
  • Maple Syrup/Vanilla: Used for sweetener, use both, one, or neither, 1 or 2 tablespoons depends on how sweet you want it
  • Pumpkin Spice: Can be bought from most grocery stores, a teaspoon or so added for flavor and decoration

Instructions:

  1. Begin by heating up your milk of choice.
  2. Pour your coffee into your cup of choice and mix in your pumpkin puree, maple syrup, pumpkin pie spice, and vanilla until well combined.
  3. Froth your milk until it is foamy, which usually takes around thirty seconds.
  4. Pour your frothed milk on top of your coffe-pumpkin mixture.
  5. Add pumpkin spice on top for a little decoration and flavor.
